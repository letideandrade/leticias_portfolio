    Welcome to the Digital Illustration section of my portfolio!

    This section of the portfolio is specifically dedicated to Digital Illustration. Most of the artworks were developed using Photoshop and/or Procreate as the main tools. Some illustration projects incorporated basic concepts of 2D animation. The process of these projects is usually recorded because itâ€™s enjoyable to watch a blank page turn into art! The video recordings last only 30 seconds, making them useful for showing clients the entire development. Human illustrations can take up to 14 hours to finalize, but even longer projects will still be summarized into the most important art frames and compiled into a 30-minute time-lapse video. All art projects are created on high-resolution canvases to ensure professional-quality illustrations, allowing for fine detail without worrying about pixelation, which is crucial for producing high-quality prints or digital media. Depending on the project's requirements, Adobe Illustrator is also used when vector art is needed, to create precise and scalable artwork. Have fun watching the videos of the projects!
